# icon-step-watchy
simple watchy face that tracks goals and rotates images

I am not a programmer, or a very amateur one, so don't be too harsh.

I have had a watchy a few years now and previously I had made some mods to starfield.

I thought I would try one from scratch because in my current job, I have lots of time that my brain in standby mode. Here are the features:

Easy to read time in 12 hour format.

Date

Day of the week highlighted.

Steps count

Step goal meter

weekdays are circled(with a square) each day that you succeed in your goal.

7 segment battery graph

10 image "icon" register that changes every five minutes(these are 150x120 pixel) You will want to change them likely!

ability to scroll images using right up/down buttons


Hope you like it!
